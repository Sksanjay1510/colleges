BACHELOR OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION(BBA)

With tremendous growth in business with barely any geographical boundaries, emerging opportunities globally have determined the way things ought to be done and has increased the demand for management professionals.

BACHELORS OF COMPUTER APPLICATION (B C A)

The Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) is multidisciplinary four-year programme as per NEP-2020. The primary goal of the BCA programme is to empower students with the requisite knowledge and abilities to pursue fulfilling professions in the rapidly evolving field of Information and Technology.
